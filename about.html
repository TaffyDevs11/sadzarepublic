About
Sadza
Republic

Founded in 2025 and proudly based in Rzeszów, Sadza Republic is a culinary brand on a mission to deliver the authentic taste of home. Specializing in traditional Zimbabwean meals with a modern twist, we’re known for our signature Sadza dishes—paired with rich pork stew, tender beef, chicken, sausage, and maguru, all served with fresh, wholesome vegetables.

Our dedication to quality, unique flavors, and relentless innovation sets us apart. Whether you're craving comfort food or discovering African cuisine for the first time, Sadza Republic is your go-to for mouthwatering meals and fast, reliable home delivery across Rzeszów and Lublin.
